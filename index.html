Privacy Policy

Nexora - School Management App

Developed and operated by Nexora Education Labs

Effective Date: March 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Nexora Education Labs ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Nexora school management application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when schools, their staff, students, and parents use the Nexora App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App immediately.

2. Who We Are

Nexora Education Labs is the developer and operator of the Nexora school management platform. We provide the App as a service to educational institutions (referred to as "Schools" or "Clients"). Each School that uses our App is a separate client with its own isolated data environment.

Developer: Nexora Education Labs
Email: nexoraeducationlabs@gmail.com

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Provided by Schools

  • Student info: Name, class, roll number, admission year.
  • Parent info: Names and contact phone numbers.
  • Staff info: Name, title, class assigned, phone/email
  • Academic data: Timetables, attendance, assignments, and exam results.

3.2 Provided by Users

  • Login credentials (email and password managed via Firebase Authentication)
  • Appointment and support requests.

3.3 Collected Automatically

  • Firebase Authentication tokens and session data
  • Device information and app usage data collected by Firebase Analytics (if enabled)
  • Crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics (if enabled)

    • 4. How We Use Information

  • We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:
  • To provide core app functionality — attendance tracking, academic records, assignments, exam scheduling, results, notices, and communication between school and parents
  • To authenticate users and manage role-based access (student, parent, teacher, coordinator, exam department, admin)
  • To allow school administrators to manage staff and student records
  • To enable parents to view their child's attendance, academic progress, and communicate with the school
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the platform
  • To improve app performance and fix technical issues
  • We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or any commercial profiling purposes.

    • 5. Data Storage and Security

    All data is stored securely on Google Firebase (Firestore),a cloud platform operated by Google LLC. Firebase provides industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest.

    Each school's data is logically isolated within our Firestore database. Users can only access data relevant to their role and their school. Access is enforced through Firebase Security Rules and Firebase Authentication.

    We implement the following security measures:

    Role-based access control — students, parents, teachers, coordinators, exam department staff, and admins each have strictly defined permissions

    Firestore Security Rules that prevent unauthorized read or write access

    No sharing of one school's data with another school or any third party

    6. Data Sharing

    We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party. We use the following trusted third-party services to operate the App:

    Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, Storage) — for database, authentication, and file storage. Firebase is governed by Google's Privacy Policy (policies.google.com/privacy)

    No other third parties have access to your data. We will only disclose data if required by law or a valid legal process.

    7. Children's Privacy

    The Nexora App is used within educational institutions and may be accessed by students who are minors. We take children's privacy seriously.

    Student accounts are created exclusively by school administrators — students and parents cannot self-register. Student data is limited to academic and attendance information necessary for school management. We do not collect unnecessary personal data from minors, and we do not use student data for any purpose beyond providing the App's educational management features.

    If you are a parent and believe your child's data is being handled inappropriately, please contact us immediately at nexoraeducationlabs@gmail.com.

    8. Data Retention

    Data is retained for the duration of the school's active subscription with Nexora Education Labs. Schools manage their own data and may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

    When a school's account is terminated, all associated data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days.

    9. Your Rights

    Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

    • Right to access: request a copy of the data we hold about you
    • Right to correction: request correction of inaccurate data
    • Right to deletion: request deletion of your data (subject to school administrator approval)
    • Right to restrict processing: request that we limit how we use your data

    10. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated effective date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

    For significant changes, we will notify school administrators directly.
